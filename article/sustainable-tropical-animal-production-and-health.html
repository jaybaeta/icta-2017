Sustainable Tropical Animal Production and Health

Topics

Tropical fisheries resources, aquaculture, postharvest & fish processing, animal feed & nutrition, animal production system, animal product technology, animal genetic & reproduction, animal diseases that impact food security, food safety & veterinary public health.

Publication

The accepted papers will be submitted in journals that are indexed by Scopus or DOAJ:

  1. Buletin Peternakan
  2. Jurnal Sain Veteriner
